Access Member Tracking:
  1. Navigate to: /community/{communityId}/members
  2. Click "Show Analytics" button
  3. View real-time member growth statistics

View Member Profile:
  1. From members list, click member name or avatar
  2. OR click "View Profile" in actions menu (⋮)
  3. See detailed profile and activity stats

Routes:
  /community/{id}/members                     → Members list with tracking
  /community/{id}/members/{memberId}          → Individual member profile
